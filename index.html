Privacy Notice

Your Data, Your Control.

CarbonWise AI is designed with an offline-first privacy architecture. By default, all carbon calculations, assessments, goals, and challenge completions are stored locally in your browser's localStorage database.

If you choose to enable the optional Firebase integration, your data will be synchronized securely with your designated Google Firebase Firestore database using your anonymous Profile Sync ID. No personal identification details (such as names, passwords, or emails) are ever requested, stored, or processed by CarbonWise AI.

You can inspect, export, or completely delete your local and cloud records at any time using the Settings panel.

About CarbonWise AI

CarbonWise AI is a premium client-side carbon tracking and education dashboard.

Our calculation factors are sourced directly from established environmental frameworks, including the US Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) emissions databases and the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) conversion guidelines.

By allowing users to run interactive simulation models, they can instantly visualize how switching to renewables, reducing flight schedules, or altering dietary practices contributes to localized climate goals.
